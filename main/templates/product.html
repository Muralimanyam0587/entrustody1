Entrustody's streamlined ecosystem lets advisors spend more time on what really matters.

Advisors know that a key driver of organic growth is their ability to provide clients with high levels of service and quality financial advice. Yet most of an advisor’s time is spent on administrative and maintenance tasks. Legacy custodial platforms create significant friction points slowing advisors down, whether through increased time generating and reconciling reports, or moving workflow through various add-ons in the technology stack.

Significant technology costs for third-party integrations force advisors to either choose suboptimal technology or trim costs in other areas of the business. Advisors on Entrustody have more time and resources to pursue their core mission of advising clients.

We focus only on what advisors require

Our platform will enable advisors through a focused end-to-end system with key integrations and an open architecture. We help advisors create the unified technology workflow that is optimized for their firm’s practices.

Maximizing operational efficiency

Entrustody is designed for advisors to quickly and effectively manage the range of back-office tasks. From seamless digital onboarding of new and existing clients, to auto-rebalancing and custom reporting, our platform helps advisors stay focused on client-facing activities.

A complete and cutting-edge investing solution

Create unique risk profiles for every portfolio. Entrustody takes in portfolio variables such as investment timeframe, target portfolio value, portfolio focus and more. Utilizing these parameters, the portfolio is customized for every client, every time.
